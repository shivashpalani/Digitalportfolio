ABOUT US

Most. Rev. Dr. Antony Anandarayar, Archbishop of Puducherry and Cuddalore, following the footsteps of Rev. Fr. Thomas Gavan Duffy, committed himself for the cause of the education of the poor that they may have a better life. He is the principal cause and driving force to start St. Ann’s College of Arts and Science [Co-Ed.] at Tindivanam to mark the 125th birth Anniversary (2015 – 2016) of Rev.Fr.Thomas Gavan Duffy. From the academic year 2015 – 2016, the college started functioning in the temporary building(RCTTI Campus) Tindivanam, with five following Under Graduate courses (186 students and 13 faculty members). From July 4th of 2016, the college is housed in the present new building with the adequate infrastructure and facilities.

  1. BA. English – English Medium
  2. BC.A. – English Medium
  3. B.Com – English Medium
  4. B.Sc. Computer Science – English Medium
  5. B.Sc. Mathematics – English Medium
